How to Use My Vault Inu

🔐 Encrypt Mode:

  • Type or paste your confidential text in the main box.
  • Enter two strong passwords — both are required for recovery.
  • Click Generate to create an encrypted recovery blob.
  • Copy and store this blob safely (e.g., in a secure note or offline file).

🔓 Decrypt Mode:

  • Switch to the Decrypt tab.
  • Paste your encrypted blob into the text area.
  • Enter both passwords exactly as before.
  • Click Recover to reveal your original secret.

⚠️ Important: If you lose either password, your data cannot be recovered. My Vault Inu uses military-grade encryption (AES-GCM + PBKDF2) — even we can’t unlock it for you.
